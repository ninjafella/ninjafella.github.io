Contract work for the Chartered Institute of Logistics and Transport in the UK (CILT)

CILT is chartered institute of logistics and transport, a 100 year not for profit educational charity with a royal charter. Princess Anne is the patron.

The department is the Awarding Organization (AO) and is governed by OFQAUL and the examinations and grades are of similar level to a university.

I had worked with CILT to create some bespoke software for one of there staff. They required a portable report generator that could be easily ran from any of their staff laptops without the need for installation.

Normally they manually make each report for all the different exam centres they have across the UK. This can take a while to do each individual report when the data is spread across separate excel spreadsheets.

Based off their needs I created a program that reads the data from the excel spreadsheets which is stored in complex tables, process this data so that the exam centres and their respective students are sorted together. All reports are then generated based on a template allowing for quick and easy changes to be made to the format of the final report using. All reports are created as word docs and PDFs so that they can be shared quickly with the test centres or allow simple changes to be made from the word docs.

The final program turned hours of manual work into running a program and waiting 1-2 minutes for it to process and generated all the reports. CILT and the people that use the program were all incredibly happy with one describing it as “Fantastic”.
